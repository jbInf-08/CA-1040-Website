Understanding Hereditary Cancer Syndromes

What are Hereditary Cancer Syndromes?

Hereditary cancer syndromes are genetic conditions that significantly increase an individual's risk of developing certain types of cancer due to inherited gene mutations.

Key Genetic Mutations

  • BRCA1/BRCA2: Associated with breast, ovarian, and prostate cancers
  • Lynch Syndrome Genes: Linked to colorectal and endometrial cancers
  • TP53 Mutations: Connected to Li-Fraumeni syndrome

Genetic Counseling and Testing

Genetic counseling helps individuals understand their inherited cancer risk, potential genetic testing options, and preventive strategies.
