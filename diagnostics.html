Molecular Diagnostics in Cancer Genomics

Biomarker Detection

Molecular diagnostics play a crucial role in identifying specific biomarkers that help in cancer diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment selection.

Genetic Screening Technologies

Advanced molecular techniques like PCR, Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), and Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization (FISH) enable precise genetic analysis.

Personalized Medicine

By understanding genetic mutations and molecular profiles, clinicians can develop targeted treatment strategies tailored to individual patients.

Key Molecular Diagnostic Techniques

  • Immunohistochemistry (IHC)
  • Fluorescence In Situ Hybridization (FISH)
  • Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)
  • Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS)
  • Liquid Biopsy
