Key Genes in Cancer Development

Oncogenes and Tumor Suppressor Genes

Some genes play critical roles in cancer development and progression. Understanding these genes is crucial for diagnosis and targeted therapies.

TP53

A critical tumor suppressor gene, often mutated in various cancers. Known as the "guardian of the genome".

BRCA1/2

Genes associated with hereditary breast and ovarian cancer. Mutations increase cancer risk significantly.

RAS

Oncogenes involved in cell signaling. Mutations can lead to uncontrolled cell growth.

EGFR

Receptor tyrosine kinase often mutated in lung and other cancers, important for targeted therapies.
