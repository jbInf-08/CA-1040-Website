Genomic Research Tools and Databases

Genomic Sequence Databases

  • NCBI GenBank

    Comprehensive public repository of genomic sequences and annotations.

    Visit Website
  • Ensembl Genome Browser

    Automated genome annotation and comparative genomics platform.

    Visit Website

Cancer-Specific Databases

  • TCGA (The Cancer Genome Atlas)

    Comprehensive multi-dimensional cancer genomics dataset.

    Visit Website
  • COSMIC (Catalog of Somatic Mutations in Cancer)

    World's largest and most comprehensive resource of somatic mutations in human cancer.

    Visit Website

Mutation Analysis Tools

  • ANNOVAR

    Tool for annotating genetic variants from high-throughput sequencing data.

    Visit Website
  • MutationAssessor

    Predicts functional impact of protein missense mutations.

    Visit Website

Research Best Practices

  1. Always cross-reference data from multiple databases
  2. Keep track of database version and date of data retrieval
  3. Validate computational predictions with experimental data
  4. Stay updated on the latest genomic research and tools
