Research and Educational Resources

A comprehensive collection of resources for researchers, educators, and students interested in cancer genomics and precision medicine.

Clinical Trials and Research Initiatives

  • NCI MATCH Study: A national precision medicine trial using a basket trial approach to match patients with targeted therapies based on genetic abnormalities [0]
  • TAPUR Study: Evaluating clinical outcomes of genomically-guided treatments [0]
  • NCI Exceptional Responders Initiative: Studying molecular characteristics of tumors associated with exceptional treatment responses [0]

Genomic Databases and Tools

  • GenBank: Comprehensive public database of DNA sequences [0]
  • PubMed: Database of biomedical literature [0]
  • The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA): Collection of genomic data from various cancer types [0]

Educational Materials and Courses

  • CGC Educational Resources: Comprehensive materials for enhancing understanding in clinical cancer genomics [0]
  • Coursera - Genomics Specialization: Online courses covering various aspects of cancer genomics
  • edX - Genomics Courses: Introduction to cancer genomics and precision medicine

Professional Organizations and Networks

  • American Society of Clinical Oncology (ASCO): Professional organization for oncologists and cancer researchers
  • American Association for Cancer Research (AACR): Organization dedicated to advancing cancer research
  • Cancer Genome Consortium (CGC): International collaboration focused on cancer genome characterization [0]

Patient Advocacy Groups

  • American Cancer Society (ACS): Comprehensive cancer information and support [0]
  • National Cancer Institute (NCI): Government agency providing cancer information and research funding
  • Patient Advocate Foundation: Organization offering patient advocacy services
